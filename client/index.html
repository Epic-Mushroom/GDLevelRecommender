GD Level Recommender
using the power of math and flooding the gddl api to help you find levels to beat
Please expect around 30-60 seconds for recommendations to appear
What is this? This is a tool that tries to help you find levels you might enjoy! How do I use this? First, you'll need to have a GDDL account and have a few submissions on record. Then, just enter your username up above, indicate a tier range if desired, and hit "Go"! How does this work? Recommendations are found through a couple of steps. First, the tool will gather all of your enjoyment ratings. Then, it'll try to find users who have similar taste in levels as you, i.e. people who give high enjoyment ratings to the levels you like and low enjoyment ratings to the ones you don't. Finally, it'll scan through these users' ratings and score levels they've rated based on their similarity to you. The highest scored levels will then be displayed! These recommendations suck! This tool uses a very small portion of GDDL data to work with to avoid rate limits. As a result, you may occasionally find levels you hate just because one compatible user happened to rate them highly. It's also good to consider that even with more data, this engine still wouldn't be perfect since whether or not you like a level is only up to you.
